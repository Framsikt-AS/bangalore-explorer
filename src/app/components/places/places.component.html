Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

October to February offers pleasant weather for sightseeing in Bangalore.

Local Transportation

Use Metro, BMTC buses, or ride-hailing apps like Ola and Uber to get around.

Language

Kannada is the official language, but English and Hindi are widely spoken.

Safety

Bangalore is generally safe, but stay vigilant in crowded places as in any major city.
